A Review of Heuristics and Hybrid Methods for Green Vehicle Routing Problems considering Emissions

Alejandro Fernandez Gil, Eduardo Lalla-Ruiz, Mariam Gomez Sanchez, and Carlos Castro

Journal of Advanced Transportation, Volume 2022, Article ID 5714991, 38 pages

DOI: 10.1155/2022/5714991

Open PDF Publisher page

Overview

This article reviews the landscape of green vehicle routing problems and focuses on the heuristic and hybrid solution methods used to address them. It organizes key problem variants, discusses how emissions and environmental considerations are modeled, and summarizes practical algorithmic strategies for obtaining strong solutions within reasonable computational time.

Why This Paper Matters

  • It offers a structured entry point to the green vehicle routing literature.
  • It highlights heuristic and hybrid approaches that remain useful when exact methods become expensive.
  • It helps researchers identify modeling patterns, benchmark directions, and open research opportunities.

Keywords

Green vehicle routing problems; heuristics; hybrid methods; emissions-aware optimization; sustainable logistics.
