A Review of Heuristics and Hybrid Methods for Green Vehicle Routing Problems considering Emissions

Alejandro Fernandez Gil, Eduardo Lalla-Ruiz, Mariam Gomez Sanchez, and Carlos Castro

Journal of Advanced Transportation (Wiley), Volume 2022, Article ID 5714991, 38 pages

DOI: 10.1155/2022/5714991

Open PDF Publisher page Alejandro ORCID

Abstract

Road freight transport is one of the logistics sectors with the highest fuel consumption and greenhouse gas emissions. This review examines green vehicle routing problems as a growing research direction at the intersection of routing optimization and environmental sustainability. It surveys heuristic and hybrid methods designed to obtain high-quality solutions in reasonable computational time, analyzes how emissions are modeled across problem variants, and maps the benchmark instances proposed in the literature.
