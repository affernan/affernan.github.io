Research

My work focuses broadly on Operations Research, Computational Intelligence Algorithms for Logistics, and Optimization in Renewable Energy Systems. I develop mathematical models and artificial intelligence techniques for sustainable transportation and scheduling problems. Much of my current work is motivated by cutting-edge approaches to mitigating GHG emissions, which have driven transformative advancements in electric aircraft and battery infrastructure planning. I am also broadly interested in integrating optimization models, geographic information systems, and computational development frameworks to tackle critical challenges related to clean energy and green hydrogen generation.
