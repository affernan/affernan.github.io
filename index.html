I am a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Ingram School of Engineering at Texas State University. My research focuses on optimization, sustainable transportation, green vehicle routing, charging and battery-swapping systems, and energy transition planning.

Research Snapshot

I work on mathematically grounded models and practical algorithms for transportation and energy systems, with particular interest in environmentally aware routing, infrastructure planning, and decision support under operational constraints.

  • Optimization for electric and green mobility systems
  • Vehicle routing with environmental and time constraints
  • Planning of hydrogen and renewable energy value chains
  • Exact methods, metaheuristics, and hybrid solution strategies
