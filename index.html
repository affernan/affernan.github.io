Research experience

Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María, Valparaíso
UTFSM | 2017 - Present

I research as a PhD student in Informatics Engineering under the direction of Ph.D. Carlos Castro, currently my focus is on environmental considerations on those classic vehicle routing problems (TSP, mTSP and VRP). My research goals is to develop matheuristic algorithms (integration of heuristic and exact algorithms) for addressing GVRPs. Currently, I am investigating the environmental and economic compensation that occur when vehicle routing is carried out efficiently, and I am also investigating the elevation factor of the routes in environmental problems.

University of Twente, Enschede
UT | 2019 - Present

My PhD project is inserted in a scientific collaboration of the Department of Informatics Engineering (UTFSM, Chile) with the Department of Business Information Systems and Industrial Engineering (UT, The Netherlands), under the co-direction of Ph.D. Eduardo Lalla-Ruiz. My main research objectives are based on the development of optimization algorithms for routing problems considering the environmental problems.
